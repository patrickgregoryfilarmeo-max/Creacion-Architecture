Company Brief

Creacion Architecture is a design-driven firm committed to delivering thoughtful and well-planned architectural solutions across a wide range of projects.

Design rooted in clarity, function, and lasting value.

Company Brief

Creacion Architecture, established in 2025, is composed of architects who earned a degree from the University of Santo Tomas (UST), one of the leading architecture schools in the Philippines.

The firm specializes in comprehensive services including planning, design, and construction supervision. From residential homes to commercial developments and selected institutional spaces, each project is approached with a focus on functionality, efficiency, and lasting value.

Company Origins

The name Creacion is derived from the Spanish word "Creacion," meaning "to create," inspired by the Philippines' rich Spanish heritage where architecture, craftsmanship, and thoughtful planning helped shape timeless spaces and communities.

Every project is guided by a balance of comfort and functionality because great architecture should make everyday living easier and more enjoyable.

Vision

Thoughtful, functional, and inspiring spaces.

To be a trusted architectural firm recognized for creating spaces that enhance everyday living and contribute to better communities. Creacion Architecture envisions shaping environments that are visually refined, meaningful, efficient, and designed to improve the way people live, work, and connect.

Mission

Helping homeowners bring dream projects to life.
