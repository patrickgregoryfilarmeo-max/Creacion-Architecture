From Vision to Reality: Our Process

A clear architectural process that moves from consultation and site study to design development, documentation, and construction guidance.

I

Client Consultation and Interview

This is the first step after contract signing, where we begin understanding your project in detail. You share your preferences, needs, lifestyle requirements, design style, and ideas you would like incorporated. In response, we provide initial insights and recommendations to help shape a clear direction for your project.

II

Initial Site Inspection

This phase is where thoughtful ideas begin to take shape. We translate your requirements into clear visual and spatial concepts through plans and 3D presentations, guided by site measurements, surroundings, site conditions, and necessary tests.

  • Site analysis
  • Inspection of site conditions
  • Measuring and assessment
  • Testing when necessary
III

Conceptual and Schematic Design Phase

This is one of the most important parts of the process, where ideas are formed. 3D perspectives, walkthroughs, conceptual plans, and other applicable media are used to communicate ideas to the client.

We collaborate closely with the client during this phase to understand requirements and objectives, then create a preliminary design concept that responds to those needs.

IV

Design Phase Outputs

Schemes

Early options that clarify the project direction.

Schematic Plans

Plans and layouts that organize space and flow.

Visual Media

Sketches, 3D perspectives, walkthroughs, and interior panoramas when requested.

V

Design Development Phase

This is the phase where technical drawings are made for the proposed design. Materials, systems, equipment, and detailed design begin to represent the completed project.

Architectural Plans

  • Site development plan
  • Floor plans, elevations, and sections
  • Details when requested
  • Cost estimate, bill of materials, and specifications

Engineering Plans

  • Plumbing plans
  • Electrical plans
  • Structural plans
  • Mechanical, fire protection, electronic, and auxiliary plans when needed
VI

Contract Documents

This phase involves preparing detailed drawings, specifications, and written documents that guide the construction process and provide the contractor with instructions to build according to the approved design.

VII

Contract Administration

During construction, we oversee the project to help ensure it is built according to approved plans and specifications. We coordinate with the contractor and client, monitor progress, and help keep the project aligned with time, budget, and quality expectations.
